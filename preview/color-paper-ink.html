Paper as default surface

The site lives on warm cream. The chapter reader switches to slate-950 (cinema mode) so the comic pages — printed for cream — pop hard against deep ink. The two surfaces are deliberately separated.

Slate is reserved for the EpisodeReader. Marketing pages, character profiles, lore, store all use paper.
